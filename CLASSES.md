# PAPER & DICE — Classes

Fourteen classes. Every one of them gives you four things and nothing else:

- **HP Base** — how much punishment you can take.
- **Ability priority** — where to put your 8 points, in order. The rest is up to you.
- **A Domain** — the slice of the world you can invent Effects about.
- **A Signature** — the one trick that is yours from level 1.

At **level 3** you pick a subclass. It narrows your Domain (Effects inside it cost **−1 Energy**) and hands you a **Rule Breaker** — one thing you do that the rules otherwise say you can't.

**There is no spell list and no ability list.** You describe what you want. The Story God prices it with the table in `RULES.md` §10. Every *"You say"* table below is examples only — not a menu. They exist to show you what things tend to cost.

## FRONT LINE

### 1. Knight

> *You are not the strongest person in the room. You are the one who has done this before.*

**Plays like** — the dependable one. You have an answer to almost every fight and you never run out of it. Where the Barbarian breaks a problem and the Rogue avoids it, you take it apart on purpose. The easiest class to play well and the hardest to play badly — and the class whose subclasses change the most about how you actually play, from pure swordwork to inscribed magic to commanding a line.

**HP Base** 12 · **Ability priority** STR *or* DEX → CON → WIS
**Domain** — *Steel, the body, and the shape of a battlefield.*
**Kit** — any weapon, medium armor, a shield, a soldier's pack.

> **Signature — Maneuver.** Once per turn, when you hit, add something for free: shove them into the next zone, disarm them, knock them Prone, or pin them where they stand. No Energy. No roll.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I kick the table into his knees and step in after it." | 1 |
| "I hook his shield away and put him on the floor." | 1 |
| "I hold this doorway. Nothing gets past me this round." | 2 |
| "I cut the rope — the chandelier comes down on the whole pack." | 2 |
| "I throw the spear hard enough to pin him to the far wall." | 3 |

#### Weapon Master
*Every weapon is one you have already used.*

**Domain** — anything with an edge, a point, or a weight. Held, thrown, or picked up off the floor.
**Rule Breaker — Perfect Grip.** Your Maneuver fires on a **miss** as well as a hit.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I flip the dagger and put it through the lock." | 1 |
| "I grab whatever's on the weapon rack and fight with both." | 1 |
| "The throw goes through his drawing hand, across the room." | 2 |

#### Warden
*The line holds because you are standing on it.*

**Domain** — shields, chokepoints, cover, and ground you refuse to give.
**Rule Breaker — Bulwark.** Enemies cannot leave your zone unless they beat you in a STR or DEX contest.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I brace the door with my own body. It holds." | 1 |
| "I drag her out of the blast and take it instead." | 1 |
| "Nobody comes down this corridor. Not this round, not the next." | 2 |

#### Duelist
*One on one, you have never lost.*

**Domain** — timing, footwork, feints, and the space between two blades.
**Rule Breaker — First Blood.** When exactly one enemy is present, you act first in initiative. Always.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I've watched him swing twice. I know what the third one is." | 1 |
| "I take the sword out of his hand mid-parry." | 1 |
| "I turn the duel into a conversation, and win that instead." | 2 |

#### Enchanter Knight
*You never learned to cast. You learned to make the steel do it.*

**Domain** — runes, wards, and the metal they are cut into. Armour and blade as the spellbook.
**Rule Breaker — Inscribed.** You may store one Effect in your weapon or armour. Pay its Energy at rest, when you cut the rune; release it later as a free action, on any turn you like. One stored Effect at a time.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "The runes catch. The blade is burning now, and it stays burning." | 1 |
| "I inscribed this shield last night. Whatever hits it next, hits nothing." | 2 |
| "The armour remembers a blow I took last week. It gives it back." | 2 |

#### Banneret
*You are not the best soldier here. You are the reason the rest of them hold.*

**Domain** — command, formation, morale, and the will of people who have agreed to follow orders.
**Rule Breaker — On My Word.** Once per round, an ally who can hear you may immediately take a Move or an Action out of turn. It costs you nothing.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Second rank. Step up. Now." | 1 |
| "Nobody in this line breaks while my banner is up." | 2 |
| "Fifty militia who have never held a spear hold for one more round." | 3 |

### 2. Barbarian

> *There is a point past which pain stops being information.*

**Plays like** — a battery that discharges. You are enormous, you get bigger when you're angry, and you have very few answers that aren't violence. When the plan works you're a sledgehammer; when the plan fails you're the reason anyone survives.

**HP Base** 14 · **Ability priority** STR → CON → WIS
**Domain** — *Raw force, fury, and endurance past reason.*
**Kit** — a heavy weapon, hide armor, and a trophy from something you killed.

> **Signature — Rage.** Free action, 2 Energy. Until the scene ends or you go a full turn without attacking: **halve all incoming physical damage**, add `+CON` to your damage, and roll INT and CHA at disadvantage.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I pick him up and throw him at the other one." | 1 |
| "I put my shoulder through the door. Not the lock — the door." | 1 |
| "I grab the front rank and drag all of them backwards with me." | 2 |
| "I take the hit on purpose so she can get past." | 2 |
| "I catch the falling stone and hold the ceiling while they run." | 3 |

#### Berserker
*The anger was already there. The fight is just permission.*

**Domain** — bloodlust, terror, and momentum that does not stop.
**Rule Breaker — No Cost.** Your first Rage each scene is free.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I scream. The room decides to be somewhere else." | 1 |
| "Three zones. Through everything in between." | 2 |
| "I'm still standing. I shouldn't be. I am." | 2 |

#### Totem
*Something old walks with you, and it has opinions.*

**Domain** — beast spirits, the endurance of the wild, borrowed shapes and instincts.
**Rule Breaker — Borrowed Hide.** Each Rage, pick one: **bear** (resist *all* damage, not just physical), **wolf** (allies have advantage on anything you attack), **eagle** (move two zones a turn).

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I smell the lie on him." | 0 |
| "I run the horse into the ground, then keep going on foot." | 1 |
| "I sleep in the blizzard. It doesn't concern me." | 1 |

#### Giantblood
*Your grandmother was very large and nobody discusses it.*

**Domain** — size, reach, weight, and throwing things that should not be thrown.
**Rule Breaker — Oversized.** You swing Heavy weapons one-handed, and you can throw them to Near.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I pick up the cart. That's the club now." | 1 |
| "He's a zone away. That's still in reach." | 1 |
| "I catch the horse." | 2 |

#### Bloodrager
*The rage doesn't stay inside. It never did.*

**Domain** — fury made physical: force, shockwaves, and the air getting out of the way.
**Rule Breaker — It Comes Out.** While you are Raging, your Effects cost no Action. The rage is producing them, not you — you are just where they happen.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I hit the ground and the ground answers." | 1 |
| "The shout arrives before I do." | 2 |
| "Everything in front of me goes backwards. All of it." | 2 |

### 3. Paladin

> *You swore something. It is listening.*

**Plays like** — a weapon with a conscience attached, and the conscience has teeth. You hit hard in bursts and you keep people standing. The oath is the real class feature: it gives the Story God a lever on you, and that is a *feature* — a Paladin with nothing to lose is just a Knight in nicer armor.

**HP Base** 12 · **Ability priority** STR *or* CHA → CON → WIS
**Domain** — *Your oath, and the light or weight it carries.*
**Kit** — a warhammer or another martial weapon, heavy armor, a shield, a holy symbol you did not choose for yourself.

> **Signature — Smite.** When you hit, spend any amount of Energy: `+1d8` oath damage per Energy, up to your cap.
>
> **The Oath.** Write three tenets at level 1. Break one knowingly and you lose your Signature *and* your Rule Breaker until you atone. The Story God decides what atonement costs, and it should cost.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I pour light into her wounds until she opens her eyes." | 1 |
| "I say his name and he cannot look away from me." | 1 |
| "Nothing unholy crosses this threshold while I stand in it." | 2 |
| "My oath burns through the whole line of them." | 3 |
| "The lie is spoken aloud, and everyone in this hall hears it as a lie." | 3 |

#### Oath of Light
*You are not the light. You are what it is pointed through.*

**Domain** — radiance, healing, warding, and revealing what hides.
**Rule Breaker — Unshakable.** You and every ally in your zone are immune to Frightened.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Daylight. In here. Now." | 2 |
| "The curse comes off her. It has to go somewhere else." | 2 |
| "I can't see it. It can't hide from this." | 2 |

#### Oath of Vengeance
*Someone did something, and you have not finished about it.*

**Domain** — pursuit, retribution, and one marked enemy.
**Rule Breaker — The Mark.** Name one enemy per scene. Any attack roll of 10 or higher hits it, whatever its Defense.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I know which direction he's in. I always do." | 0 |
| "He's faster than me. He isn't going to stay ahead." | 1 |
| "The armor doesn't matter. I'm not looking for a gap." | 2 |

#### Oath of Atonement
*You did something. This is how you carry it.*

**Domain** — penance, endurance, and suffering taken on behalf of others.
**Rule Breaker — Take the Blow.** You may accept any damage aimed at an ally in your zone. It is halved.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I draw the poison out of her and into me." | 1 |
| "I stand in the fire so she doesn't have to." | 2 |
| "Whatever he did — I'll carry it. Let him go." | 3 |

#### Oath of the Silent
*No banner, no shining armour, no announcement. Judgement all the same.*

**Domain** — investigation, judgement, oaths sworn where nobody heard, and the guilty.
**Rule Breaker — The Confession.** Ask one creature one question about something it has done. It cannot lie to you. It may refuse to answer — and you will know that it refused.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Three days in this town. I know which one of them it is." | 1 |
| "He knows what he did. Now everyone in the room does too." | 2 |
| "My oath was sworn to nobody at all. It still holds." | 2 |

### 4. Monk

> *Everyone else brought a weapon. You didn't need to.*

**Plays like** — fast, slippery, precise. You go wherever you want on the battlefield and hit several times when you get there. Fragile if you stand still, so don't. The most mobile class in the game by a wide margin.

**HP Base** 10 · **Ability priority** DEX → WIS → CON
**Domain** — *The body as a weapon: breath, momentum, and stillness.*
**Kit** — simple robes, a staff or a pair of light weapons, a bowl.

> **Signature — Flow.** Your unarmed die is `d6` (`d8` at level 5, `d10` at level 9), and the Attack action gives you one **extra unarmed attack** for free. While unarmored, your **Defense is `10 + DEX + WIS`**.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I run up the wall and come down behind him." | 1 |
| "I catch the arrow." | 1 |
| "I strike a nerve. His arm stops working." | 2 |
| "I move through the whole line, hitting each of them once." | 2 |
| "I stand perfectly still and nothing they do lands." | 3 |

#### Open Hand
*Bodies are machines and you have read the manual.*

**Domain** — pressure points, joints, throws, and shutting a body down.
**Rule Breaker — Read the Body.** After you hit a creature, you know its current HP and one weakness.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "The one on the left is hurt. I can see it in how he stands." | 0 |
| "He's twice my size. He goes over my hip anyway." | 1 |
| "I strike under the ribs. His heart skips three beats." | 2 |

#### Shadow
*Stillness, then absence.*

**Domain** — darkness, silence, and being somewhere you were not.
**Rule Breaker — Step Through Dark.** 1 Energy: step from any shadow to any other within Far.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Every light in this room goes out at once." | 1 |
| "The blow comes from a direction that has nobody in it." | 1 |
| "I walk past the watchman. In the open. He doesn't register it." | 2 |

#### Four Elements
*The body is one of the four. It happens to be the one you're in.*

**Domain** — fire, water, air, and stone, channelled through the body.
**Rule Breaker — Elemental Fist.** Your unarmed attacks deal any elemental damage type you like and reach Near.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "The punch arrives as wind, from across the room." | 1 |
| "Six steps across the water. That's all I need." | 1 |
| "Stone comes up out of the floor between us." | 2 |

#### Mender
*The same knowledge that stops a heart can start one.*

**Domain** — breath, circulation, nerves, poison, and putting a body back the way it was.
**Rule Breaker — Hands On.** Your healing Effects cost no Action and need no roll — but you must be Engaged and touching them. You cannot heal at range at all.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Breathe. There. That's the poison out." | 1 |
| "I set the bone and she's back up this round." | 1 |
| "Everyone I can reach. All at once. Then I'm finished." | 3 |

### 5. Rogue

> *There is a way in. There is always a way in.*

**Plays like** — the highest single hit in the game if you set it up, and almost nothing if you don't. Outside combat you're the one who solves the problem before it becomes a fight. Rewards positioning and patience.

**HP Base** 8 · **Ability priority** DEX → CHA *or* INT → CON
**Domain** — *Angles, timing, locks, lies, and the soft parts.*
**Kit** — two light weapons, light armor, thieves' tools, and something you have not yet returned.

> **Signature — Sneak Attack.** Once per turn, when you hit with advantage, or when the target is Engaged with one of your allies: add `2d6`. Rises to `4d6` at level 5 and `6d6` at level 9.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I'm not where he swung. I'm behind him." | 1 |
| "That's his purse, this is his key, and he's noticed neither." | 1 |
| "I put out the torches and let the dark do the rest." | 2 |
| "The wine was poisoned four hours ago. This is just when it starts." | 2 |
| "I was never in this building. Check the records — I never was." | 3 |

#### Assassin
*The fight is a formality. The work happened earlier.*

**Domain** — ambush, poison, disguise, and the killing stroke.
**Rule Breaker — Killer's Opening.** Your first attack against a creature that has not yet acted in combat is automatically a critical hit. *Once per scene.*

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I've watched him for an hour. Now I'm him." | 2 |
| "The poison starts working at midnight. I chose midnight." | 2 |
| "He'll be standing there in four minutes. I know because I know him." | 1 |

#### Infiltrator
*Doors are a suggestion and you have not agreed to it.*

**Domain** — locks, wards, guards, walls, and everything meant to keep you out.
**Rule Breaker — No Such Door.** Ten uninterrupted minutes and you are inside. No roll.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "No footprints, no scent, no one was here." | 1 |
| "We leave the way we came, at walking pace." | 1 |
| "The ward stays running. I just stop matching its definition." | 2 |

#### Trickster
*The truth is whatever survives the next five minutes.*

**Domain** — misdirection, confidence games, and borrowed identities.
**Rule Breaker — It Was Always Yours.** Once per scene, declare that you lifted something plausible from someone you have been near. It's true.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I heard him speak once. That's enough." | 1 |
| "The writ is genuine. It'll survive exactly one reading." | 2 |
| "By morning each of them is certain the other sold them out." | 3 |

### 6. Ranger

> *You saw it three hours before it saw you.*

**Plays like** — control and consistency. You pick the target and suddenly it's everyone's target. Away from the fight you keep the party alive, fed, and un-lost — the class that makes travel and wilderness actually *play* instead of being narrated past.

**HP Base** 10 · **Ability priority** DEX → WIS → CON
**Domain** — *Wilds, tracks, prey, and the distance between you and it.*
**Kit** — a bow or crossbow, a melee weapon, light armor, a traveller's kit, a map you drew yourself.

> **Signature — Mark.** Free action, 1 Energy. Mark one creature you can see. You deal `+1d6` to it, and every ally has advantage tracking it. Lasts the scene. When it dies, Mark the next one free.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Four of them. One is limping. They came through last night." | 1 |
| "I shoot the rope holding the gate." | 1 |
| "One arrow, everyone in that doorway." | 2 |
| "We take this pass. I know a way that doesn't involve the pass." | 2 |
| "I've been setting up this clearing since dawn. Roll initiative." | 3 |

#### Sharpshooter
*The shot exists. You just have to wait for it.*

**Domain** — distance, wind, patience, and the perfect angle.
**Rule Breaker — No Cover.** Cover and concealment never penalise your ranged attacks.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "The chain has one weak link. That one." | 1 |
| "It's further than anyone thinks is possible. I take it anyway." | 1 |
| "Through the keyhole. Yes, really." | 2 |

#### Beast Bond
*There are two of you and only one draws a sheet.*

**Domain** — one animal, and the bond between you.
**Rule Breaker — Two Bodies.** Your companion acts on your initiative and takes its own Action free. `Level × 5` HP, attacks at your bonus for `d6`.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I send her in. I can't fit. She can." | 0 |
| "We flank him. Neither of us needed to say anything." | 0 |
| "I see what she sees. She's a mile out." | 1 |

#### Trailblazer
*You have already been here, and you left things.*

**Domain** — terrain, traps, ambush ground, and survival.
**Rule Breaker — Prepared Ground.** Ten minutes in a place and you have set traps. Declare what they do when combat starts.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "There's a storm in two days. Plan around it." | 0 |
| "The snare takes something twice my weight." | 1 |
| "The bog is passable. For exactly four of us." | 2 |

### 7. Bard

> *Nobody in the room has noticed yet that you are the one deciding what happens.*

**Plays like** — the amplifier. You rarely have the biggest number, but you hand out the die that turns someone else's failure into a success, and you talk the party out of half the fights they'd otherwise lose. The most social class, and unusually good at making everyone else feel powerful.

**HP Base** 8 · **Ability priority** CHA → DEX → CON
**Domain** — *Story, song, and what people believe about themselves.*
**Kit** — an instrument, light armor, a light weapon, and a debt someone owes you.

> **Signature — Inspiration.** Free action, 1 Energy. Hand an ally a `d6`. They may add it to any roll before the scene ends. `d8` at level 5, `d10` at level 9. One per ally at a time.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "I tell him the story of his own father and watch him decide not to swing." | 1 |
| "There's a second set of footsteps in this room. Everyone hears them." | 1 |
| "The whole tavern is singing, and nobody is watching the back door." | 2 |
| "I sing the fear out of them. All of them." | 2 |
| "By tomorrow this town remembers a very different version of tonight." | 3 |

#### Wordsmith
*You have never needed to raise your voice.*

**Domain** — persuasion, courts, rumour, negotiation, reputation.
**Rule Breaker — Word Ahead.** When you arrive somewhere new, you decide what they have already heard about you.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Give me a minute in the room. I'll know what they want." | 1 |
| "This is an execution. I'm making it a trial." | 2 |
| "The story reaches the next town before we do." | 2 |

#### Warsinger
*War has a rhythm and you are keeping it.*

**Domain** — rhythm, courage, coordination, and organised violence.
**Rule Breaker — Marching Song.** Spending your Inspiration also grants that ally a free Move.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "One horn call. My party knows exactly what it means." | 0 |
| "Six frightened farmers. They hold one more round." | 2 |
| "Everyone strikes on the beat. Now." | 3 |

#### Taleweaver
*The difference between a lie and a story is how well it's told.*

**Domain** — illusion, glamour, false memory, and things that seem.
**Rule Breaker — It Looked Real.** 1 Energy: an illusion fills a zone and engages every sense. It fails only when something touches it.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "There's an army on that ridge. There isn't." | 2 |
| "For tonight, I'm someone else entirely." | 2 |
| "This room remembers a conversation that never happened." | 3 |

## THE FOUR MAGICS

*Four classes reach the same power through four incompatible doors. What separates them is not what they can do — it's what it costs, and who is watching.*

---

### 8. Wizard — *magic that was studied*

> *It took eleven years. You have notes.*

**Plays like** — preparation rewarded. Choose well at the start of the day and you're the most efficient caster at the table; get caught outside your preparation and you're a person in a robe. The most versatile class across a whole session, and the most punishing of laziness.

**HP Base** 6 · **Ability priority** INT → CON → DEX
**Domain** — *Studied magic. Anything you have written down.*
**Kit** — a grimoire, robes, a focus, ink and vellum, and one experiment that went badly.

> **Signature — Grimoire.** On each Long Rest, prepare **three Focuses** — narrow themes from your Domain (*fire · doors · memory · weight · water*). Effects inside a prepared Focus cost **−1 Energy**.
>
> You may also cast any Effect as a **Ritual**: ten minutes, **no Energy at all** — but never during combat.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "A spark, a gust, the page turns itself." | 0 |
| "The lock remembers being open." | 1 |
| "Fire. In the doorway. All of them." | 2 |
| "I unmake the bridge behind us." | 2 |
| "That spell is not going to happen." *(as it's being cast)* | = its cost |
| "Give me ten minutes and this circle will hold anything." | 0 *(Ritual)* |

#### Illusionist
*Reality is a consensus and you hold a minority opinion.*

**Domain** — light, sound, seeming, and belief.
**Rule Breaker — Doubt.** Anyone trying to disbelieve your illusions rolls at disadvantage.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "There's a wall here. There isn't. But there is." | 1 |
| "I'm not visible until I do something about it." | 2 |
| "He believes he is burning. So he burns." | 3 |

#### Wardmaster
*Magic is mostly a question of what you don't let in.*

**Domain** — barriers, binding, banishment, and protection.
**Rule Breaker — Standing Ward.** A ward is always up. The first `Level × 4` damage you take each scene is absorbed.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Nothing crosses this circle." | 2 |
| "Whatever bound this — I'm unbinding it." | 2 |
| "Back where you came from." | 4 |

#### Diviner
*You have read ahead.*

**Domain** — knowledge, scrying, portents, and the shape of what is coming.
**Rule Breaker — Foreseen.** At the start of each session, roll a `d20` and write it down. Once, you may replace *any* roll at the table — yours, an ally's, or the Story God's — with that number.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Move. Three seconds before it mattered." | 1 |
| "I'm watching a room I'm not in." | 2 |
| "I know his true name. I've known it since Tuesday." | 3 |

#### Transmuter
*Nothing is what it is. It is only what it currently is.*

**Domain** — matter, form, weight, state, and change.
**Rule Breaker — Nothing Is Fixed.** Once per scene, permanently change one property of an object you can touch. Free.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "The floor under him is water now." | 1 |
| "That door is paper. It was oak a moment ago." | 1 |
| "The falling stone weighs twice what it did." | 2 |

### 9. Sorcerer — *magic that was inherited*

> *You did not learn this. It was already in you and it has been waiting.*

**Plays like** — raw and flexible. Your Bend lets you reshape any Effect on the fly, so you improvise better than anyone. No book to lose, no patron to answer to — just a bloodline that occasionally has ideas of its own. The most elemental class, and the one where the subclass matters most.

**HP Base** 6 · **Ability priority** CON → DEX → CHA
**Domain** — *Magic that lives in your blood. It never needed a book.*
**Kit** — whatever you were wearing when it first happened, and the mark it left.

> **Signature — Bend.** When you produce an Effect, spend **1 extra Energy** to apply one of these:
> **Twin** — a second target · **Reach** — one zone further · **Silent** — no words, no gestures · **Quick** — costs no Action · **Brutal** — maximise one damage die.
>
> **Wild Surge.** You may cast *without paying*. Roll `d20`: on **10+** it's free. On **9 or less** you pay **double** and the Story God adds a complication.

| You say | Energy |
|---|---|
| "Sparks off my hand. Just enough to be a problem." | 0 |
| "He's on fire and he doesn't know why yet." | 1 |
| "Both of them. Same instant." | 2 *(1 + Twin)* |
| "I don't say anything, I don't move, and it happens anyway." | 2 *(1 + Silent)* |
| "The whole corridor. From here." | 3 |

### 10. Warlock — *magic that was borrowed*

> *You didn't earn this and you didn't inherit it. You agreed to terms.*

**Plays like** — burst and consequence. Your Energy comes back on a **Short Rest**, so where other casters ration, you spend everything and then rest. The trade is that something out there has a claim on you, and the Story God gets to use it. The darkest class in the game, and the one that generates the most story on its own.

**HP Base** 8 · **Ability priority** WIS → CON → CHA
**Domain** — *Borrowed power. Whatever your Patron is willing to lend, and it is not always the same amount.*
**Kit** — a focus your Patron chose for you, light armor, and the terms of the agreement, if you were given a copy.

> **Signature — The Bargain.** You recover your **full Energy on a Short Rest**, not half.
>
> **The Debt.** Once per session the Story God may call it in: an instruction, a demand, a price. You may refuse — and lose your Signature and Rule Breaker until you make amends. Your Patron does not explain and does not negotiate.

### 12. Cleric

> *You are not asking. You are relaying.*

**Plays like** — the safety net with teeth. You keep people alive, you cancel the thing that was about to ruin the encounter, and once a session you get to ask the universe for a favour it cannot refuse. Steady rather than flashy — until the Miracle, which is the single most open-ended ability in the game.

**HP Base** 10 · **Ability priority** WIS → CON → STR *or* CHA
**Domain** — *Your god's portfolio, and the faith of the people around you.*
**Kit** — a holy symbol, medium armor, a shield or a staff, and a text you have read too many times.

> **Signature — Miracle.** Once per session, ask your god for something outside the rules entirely.
>
> The Story God decides what happens and what it costs — **but never says no outright.** A Miracle always does *something*. It may not be what you asked for.

### 14. Alchemist

> *Magic is chemistry that hasn't been written down properly yet.*

**Plays like** — a planner. You bank your power at rest and spend it as physical objects, which means you can hand your abilities to *other people*. The only class that can arm the whole party. Weak at improvising, extremely strong at preparing.

**HP Base** 8 · **Ability priority** INT → DEX → CON
**Domain** — *Substances, reactions, mechanisms, and what they do to a body.*
**Kit** — a portable lab, light armor, a light weapon, and a notebook of failures.

> **Signature — Brew.** On each Short or Long Rest, prepare `INT + Level` **doses**. A dose is one Effect, priced normally — but paid for at rest, not in the moment.
>
> **Anyone can use a dose.** Hand them out. Doses spoil at your next rest, so hoarding does not work.
